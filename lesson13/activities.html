Salmon River Rafting

Experience rafting at Salmon River also known as "The River of No Return" located in Idaho in the northwestern United States. It is the second deepest gorge on the American continent, and is divided into different sections, namely the Main Salmon, the Middle Fork of the Salmon River and the Lower Salmon. It is a perfect getaway for group or family. Get ready to get wet!

Salmon River Falls

The Salmon River Falls is a 110-foot (34m) waterfall. Located within the Salmon River Falls Unique Area; near the village of Altmar Oswego County. It is a popular sightseeing destination within the town of Orwell. The falls and surrounding land area are open all year-round to the public for recreation. The best part admission is free.

Salmon River Fishing

Your next best fishing adventure waits on the Salmon River, a unique opportunity to relax. Spend a day out on the Salmon River fishing and receive expert advice, tips and tricks on how to catch fish. Remember to review our tips to make the most of your fishing Idaho experience. Enjoy fishing!

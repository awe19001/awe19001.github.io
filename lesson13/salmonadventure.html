Popular Activities

Salmon Fishing
Your next best fishing adventure waits on the Salmon River, a unique opportunity to relax. Spend a day out on the Salmon River fishing and receive expert advice, tips and tricks on how to catch fish. Remember to review our tips to make the most of your fishing Idaho experience. Enjoy fishing!
Salmon River Falls
The Salmon River Falls is a 110-foot (34m) waterfall. Located within the Salmon River Falls Unique Area; near the village of Altmar Oswego County. It is a popular sightseeing destination within the town of Orwell. Enjoy the spectacular view from the upper trail. Please review our tips and guides.
Salmon Rafting
Enjoy an amazing adventure at Salmon River also known as "The River of No Return" located in Idaho in the northwestern United States. It is the second deepest gorge on the American continent. A perfect getaway for individuals, families and friends. Have fun traveling along the rushing waters.

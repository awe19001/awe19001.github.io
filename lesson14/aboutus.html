About Us

The Salmon River Adventures river rafting tour company is located in Riggins, Idaho and offers world-class, whitewater rafting experiences on the Salmon River in the middle of an Idaho wilderness. The core business values of high-end adventures, professional and experienced service, safety, and fun. We offers day and overnight trips for groups and individuals using state-of-the-art equipment and complies with all U.S. Forest Service regulations.

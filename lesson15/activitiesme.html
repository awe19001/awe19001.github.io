Salmon River Falls

The Salmon River Falls is a 110-foot (34m) waterfall. Located within the Salmon River Falls Unique Area; near the village of Altmar Oswego County. It is a popular sightseeing destination within the town of Orwell. Please review our tips and guides. Enjoy the spectacular view from the upper trail.
